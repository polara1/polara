Training
Prior to your space journey, Polara will provide you with astronaut level training. You will be trained by an experienced astronaut who will guide you through the process. The training will include adapting to the weightlessness of space, understanding of the spacecraft layout, and knowing the spacecraft system. In addition, you will need to maintain good health in order to withstand the elevated g-force during the launch.

Recovery
Welcome Home!
After returning to Earth, Polara will tow the spaceship and transport the passengers. Afterwards, you will be able to reunite with your family, friends, and the Polara team to celebrate this unique experience. This will not be the end. You will always have absolute access to the newest information and forever be a part of the amazing Polara experience. You will get to join the exclusive community of those who have had the one of a kind opportunity to discover beyond our planet.
