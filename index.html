Polara is an experience-first company that is focused on providing an unforgettable extraterrestrial journey.

At Polara we believe that safety and comfort are of utmost importance. We offer a one of a lifetime experience of traveling to space and experiencing your dreams.

Our state-of-the-art spacecraft and experienced team of experts will take you on a journey of a lifetime, giving you the opportunity to witness the awe-inspiring beauty of our planet from a unique perspective.

At Polara we also believe in making this journey possible for everyone. If you cannot make the journey in person, we offer the exclusive opportunity to take a belonging of personal value for you to space and back so that the experience can forever live on with you.

Come join us as we push the boundaries of what's possible and take humanity to new heights!
